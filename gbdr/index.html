GLOBAL BUSINESS DATABASE REGISTER

What is the "Global Business Database Register"?
The Global Business Database Register is a free-to-register business name database.
In some countries, registering a business name can be expensive. The Global Business Database Register was created as an effort to offer small business and startups a free platform to register a business name.

How does it work?
Each entry has four components - Business name, City/Country, Public key and Private key.
Business names and their registered city/country are accessible in the public domain.
Public keys are accessible in the public domain to serve as proof of registration with the Global Business Database Register.
The private key will never be shared in the public domain and is provided only to the original registrant. The private key is used for internal authentication.
In the event of a dispute over an entry and the true registrant, the private key will be used to resolve the dispute.

Who maintains the database?
The database is maintained by GC Media Publishing Management.
Database entries are accessible in the public domain, with the exception of the private key.
Registered cities and countries are assigned and authenticated with the Pitrix global allocations program.
The public list is free to access, without restriction.
The private list is restricted access.
